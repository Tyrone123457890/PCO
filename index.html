My Motivation for Choosing HBO-ICT

The HBO-ICT programme gives me practical experience that fits my passion for technology and solving problems. We’ll be working on real-world projects and collaborating with actual companies, which helps prepare us for future jobs. It also lets me explore different areas like software engineering and data science.

The programme supports creativity and innovation. I want to work in a field where I can keep learning, grow through new challenges, and help create useful solutions. HBO-ICT gives me the right tools and environment to achieve that.

Key Reasons ICT Suits Me

  • I enjoy learning new tech and staying up to date.
  • I’m creative and love building things.
  • I enjoy solving problems and finding solutions.
